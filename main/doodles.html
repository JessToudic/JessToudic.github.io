The story

  My first doodle was for a school project when I was 12 years old. This inspired me to begin doodling on the envelopes of birthday cards, and soon after, the cards themselves. Fast forward a few years, and I have created a growing digital collection: YippeeYayDoodles.

  One of my first doodles was a pot decoration (first picture). All birthday cards I give are doodled by me! The second photo is an example of one I did in the sun before work. :) I create my digital doodles using Autodesk SketchBook on my Microsoft Surface Pro, and upload them to Redbubble, where you can order my doodles on lots of items - here's a tote bag!
